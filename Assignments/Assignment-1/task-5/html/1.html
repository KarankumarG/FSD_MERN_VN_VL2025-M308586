My Top 5 Favourite Movies

  1. Inception
    • Mind-bending storyline with multiple layers of reality
    • Outstanding performance by Leonardo DiCaprio
    • Hans Zimmer’s unforgettable soundtrack
  2. The Dark Knight
    • Heath Ledger’s iconic portrayal of the Joker
    • Intense action sequences and cinematography
    • Explores deep themes of morality and chaos
  3. Interstellar
    • Visually stunning depiction of space and black holes
    • Emotional father-daughter storyline
    • Scientific concepts explained in an engaging way
  4. Avengers: Endgame
    • Epic conclusion to the Marvel saga
    • Emotional character arcs and sacrifices
    • Thrilling battle scenes with all heroes united
  5. Spirited Away
    • Beautiful hand-drawn animation by Studio Ghibli
    • Magical and imaginative world-building
    • Strong themes of courage and self-discovery
